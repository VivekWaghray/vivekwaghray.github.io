About Me

Hi! I'm Vivek Waghray. I'm a senior computer science major and general business minor at the University of Maryland. Most of who I am consists of my childhood in America and my experience in India. I've moved 5 times and one of those times consisted of living in India for 4 years. Having moved so much I've learned to adapt and handle different atmospheres. I am also part of the QUEST Honors Program and UMD Park Scholars Public Leadership (PL) program. QUEST is a multi-disciplinary consulting program in which teams consisting of 5 students do a real-world consulting project. This experience has taught me a lot and helped me hone my skills with regards to adaptability, leadership, communication, and handling various difficult tasks in a short time. Through my time in PL, I have gained a better understanding of proper leadership skills and how to apply them efficiently. Along with that, I've improved my communication and speaking skills and expanded my network.
